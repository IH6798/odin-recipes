Lasagna Recipe

Description

Lasagna is a hearty Italian dish made of layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted cheese. The meat sauce is made with ground beef or pork, tomatoes, and spices, while the béchamel sauce is a smooth, creamy white sauce. Layers of these components are assembled, then baked until golden and bubbly

Lasagna comes from Italy people say, but who knows? People steal food from other countries all the time since day 0 so maybe it's not actually Italian...

Ingredients

  • lasagna noodles
  • ground beef
  • tomatoes
  • cheese
  • pasta sauce

Steps

  1. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions and set aside
  2. Brown the ground beef in a pan, then add tomatoes, salt, pepper, and spices to make the meat sauce
  3. Prepare the béchamel sauce by melting butter, whisking in flour, then adding milk until thickened
  4. In a baking dish, layer the meat sauce, noodles, ricotta, mozzarella, and béchamel sauce. Repeat layers
  5. Top with Parmesan and bake at 375°F (190°C) for 25-30 minutes until bubbly and golden. Let it rest before serving
