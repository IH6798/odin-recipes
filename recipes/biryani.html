Biryani Recipe

Description

Biryani is a flavorful rice dish made by layering spiced rice and marinated meat (like chicken). Marinate the chicken with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, spices, and herbs. Cook basmati rice with whole spices until 70% done.

Layer the partially cooked rice and chicken in a pot, adding fried onions, mint, cilantro, and saffron milk (optional). Seal and cook on low heat for 20-25 minutes. Fluff the rice and serve with raita or salad.

Ingredients

  • basmati rice
  • yoghurt
  • chicken
  • garam masala
  • onions
  • ginger
  • garlic

Steps

  1. Marinate the chicken with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, garam masala, and salt for at least 1 hour
  2. Cook the basmati rice with whole spices like bay leaves and cardamom until 70% done, then drain
  3. Saut√© the marinated chicken in oil or ghee until fully cooked
  4. Layer the chicken and rice in a pot, adding fried onions and saffron milk (optional) between layers
  5. Seal the pot and cook on low heat for 20-25 minutes. Fluff the rice and serve hot
