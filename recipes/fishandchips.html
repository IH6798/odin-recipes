Fish and Chips Recipe

Description

Fish and chips is a beloved comfort food, known for its crispy, golden coating and tender fish inside. The combination of freshly fried fish with perfectly crispy chips is irresistible, making it a popular takeaway dish in many countries. When made at home, it's even better, with the option to adjust seasoning and fry everything to your liking

The key to a great fish and chips meal is the batter's crispiness and the chips' perfect textureâ€”soft inside, crispy outside. Add a squeeze of lemon and a side of tartar sauce for an extra burst of flavor!

Ingredients

  • fish fillets
  • salt
  • potatoes
  • flour
  • baking powder

  1. Peel and cut the potatoes into thick chips, then fry in hot oil until golden and crispy
  2. For the batter, mix flour, baking powder, salt, and beer (or sparkling water) until smooth
  3. Coat the fish fillets in flour, dip them into the batter, and fry in hot oil until crispy and golden
  4. Drain the fried fish and chips on paper towels
  5. Serve with tartar sauce and enjoy
